Introduction

Too Long in Exile

For more than a decade I have been absent from the land I love. My thoughts have never strayed far from the rugged ochre outback and the snaking golden coastline. In my time overseas the words have in my head have accumulated. Thoughts on justice, equality, humanity have queued quietly and bode their moment.

As the world reassesses policy priorities it has become time for me to speak. It is time to set my voice in the spotlight. I have taken action, steps towards understanding the contractions in the great southern land. My words are placed here for all to see. Each sentence edges me ever nearer to home. Every paragraph considered an Australia where there is an optimistic hope for tomorrow's heroes. If anyone is willing to listen.
